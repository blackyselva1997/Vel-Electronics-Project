WHO WE ARE

S.Velmurugan. M.D.,

Mobile : +91 9659130094
E-mail : inframurugans@gmail.com

I am VELMURUGAN the sole founder of Vel electronics; it is professionally set-upped sole proprietary manufacturing cum service industry started in 2015 based on Tenkasi district, Tamilnadu. I did my UG BE-Electrical and Electronics Engineering in Einstein College of Engineering, and PG ME- Computer Science and Engineering in Regional Centre of Anna University, at Tirunelveli in Tamilnadu. In early my childhood days I was so interested on playing with wild matter like wind, rain water and sun rays. Sometimes I tried to convert that natural power into useful way by making toy mechanism. Then later periods I understood one think that “if I would do natural power in useful ways, professionally I would expert in that field”. So I choose ‘electrical and electronics engineering’ in my bachelor course in Einstein college of Engineering, there I learn how to convert one form of energy in to another one form.

After my college days I started to learn embedded technology when I went to work as apprentice in NLC India limited at Neyveli in Tamilnadu. After that in 2011-12 I went to work as embedded system programmer with M/s Ellcrafts at Poonamallee in Chennai. Then Solely I started my profession by doing embedded project for engineering student with few year experiences in embedded technology. When I was doing his post graduate in 2013 in the name of “VEL ELECTRONICS” I applied for District Industrial Centre (DIC) to get recognition to my profession, here I say my heartfelt thanks to DIC manager madam Mrs.Mariammal B.Tech., MBA., for giving wonderful guidance to me.

As continuous of that in 2014 the district collector’s entrepreneur task force committee selected me as a beneficiary in new entrepreneur cum enterprise development scheme (NEEDS). After I completed one month entrepreneurship training program in Madurai by Guindy Entrepreneurship Development Institute, I got an entrepreneurship Certificate.

Then all proposals sent to Tamilnadu Grama Bank, Venkateswarapuram branch in Tenkasi district by district industrial center DIC. Then I met the branch manager Mr.Navamani Jebadurai sir for my SME loan, he appreciate my approach and sanction the loan to me to set-up my industry in 2015. Here I say my heartfelt thanks to branch manager and regional manager Mr.Sankili Pandi sir for gave me the loan. Now I am successfully running my industry with around ten employees.

Vel electronics is an electronics product manufacturing industry that currently running in Venkateswarapuram, Tirunelveli district. It has highly educated professionals for management & research and well trained & experienced employees for manufacturing & service.

We strictly follow on dignity and quality in each and every aspect of our company. Our company slogan says two things, that’s

  • Developing the electricity from renewable & sustainable energy resources
  • Reducing the loss of power in electricity by advanced embedded controls

The major part of our product in particular, comparative performance is based on sustainable green energies and advanced technologies that reduce the loss of power. Currently we are manufacturing LED bulbs, AC voltage divider, embedded controls for street light and motor pump sets and Charger for rechargeable battery items. We also provide solar photovoltaic power systems for Homes, Shops and industries. The management would try to carry out this company beyond the industry average with global competitive environment.
